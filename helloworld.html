Hello World!

Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM | Category: Personal

A little bit about me, and a little bit about this blog. I've never done anything like this before, so bear with me.

I am Jordan Joaquin Lopez. I am a programmer, a computer scientist, a student, and a huge geek. I am in my second year at the University of Texas at Austin, getting my bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I am loving it here, there are so many things to learn, and so many talented and amazing people to learn from.

This is my first blog, and I am tentatively calling it The Whiteboard. I'm hoping to fill it with all kinds of interesting, computer-science-y stuff. They say the best way to learn is to teach, so I'm going to try and be as educational as I can with my content. We'll see how it goes, I'm excited.
